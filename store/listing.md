Merge Royal — Merge to Win!

Slide, drop and merge cards with the same number to climb from 2 all the way to 4096 and beyond. Merge Royal is a fast, satisfying neon card puzzle that blends classic 2048 math with a clever card-stacking twist — easy to learn, impossible to put down.

HOW TO PLAY
Drag a card onto a matching card to merge it and double its value. Build staircases (2, 4, 8, 16…) and trigger chain reactions for X2, X3 and even bigger score multipliers. Keep your columns from overflowing, spend your mistakes wisely and push on to the next level.

FEATURES
• One-finger drag & drop — simple to play, deep to master
• Satisfying cascade merges with combo multipliers
• Level up with rising challenge and rewards
• Special suit cards (♠ ♥ ♣ ♦ ♛) that boost your score, refund a mistake or grant power-ups
• Bomb to blast away locked cards, and Shuffle to rescue a stuck board
• Slick neon visuals with juicy effects and sound
• Auto-save — pick up and continue anytime
• 100% offline · No account · No sign-up · No data collected
